Allu Arjun

Allu Arjun (born 8 April 1982) is an Indian actor who predominantly works in Telugu films. He is one of the highest paid actors in South India and known for his dancing abilities,Allu is a recipient of several awards, including five Filmfare Awards South and five Nandi Awards.Since 2014, he has been featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list based on his income and popularity.
